Protect yourself and loved ones

Need help?

Avoid getting sick

Wash hands often with soap and water especially after using the bathroom, before eating and if hands are dirty.

If you get sick

Stay home except to get medical care, get plenty of rest and drink adequate fluid

Cover your mouth

While sneezing or coughing always cover your mouth and avoid close contact with other people
